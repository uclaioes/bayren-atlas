This project is funded by the Bay Area Regional Energy Network (BayREN), a collaboration of the nine counties that make up the San Francisco Bay Area, in partnership with the California Center for Sustainable Communities at UCLA.

Led by the Association of Bay Area Governments, BayREN provides regional-scale energy efficiency programs, services, and resources, and is funded by utility ratepayer funds through the California Public Utilities Commission, as well as through grants from member, state, and federal agencies. BayREN draws on the expertise, knowledge, and proven track record of Bay Area local governments.
